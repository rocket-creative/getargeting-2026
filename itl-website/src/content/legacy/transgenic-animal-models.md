# Transgenic Animal Models

## Mouse, Rat, and Rabbit

Ingenious targeting laboratory, a leader in custom animal model generation for over 30 years, simplifies the creation of transgenic mouse, rat, and rabbit models for your research endeavors.

Whether confirming in vitro results, advancing from in vitro studies, or developing specific transgenic models that you do not already have access to, our experienced team guides you through the process.

## Available Species

### Mouse Models
Our most comprehensive offering with the widest range of genetic modification options.

### Rat Models
Ideal for studies requiring larger animal size, including surgical manipulations and physiological studies.

### Rabbit Models
Perfect for immunology research, antibody production, and certain cardiovascular studies.

- **Reliability**: Consistent results across generations
- **Flexibility**: Available in multiple species and genetic backgrounds
- **Experience**: Over 30 years of expertise in model generation
